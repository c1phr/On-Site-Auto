Desert Mountain Fabrication

Harold founded Desert Mountain Fab as a result of his love of the outdoors, and enthusiasm for off-roading. Due to many years of modifying both his, and his friends vehicles, Harold is a master at design and fabrication. After receiveing many requests for modified parts, Desert Mountain Fab was formed to design and build parts for your off-road adventures.
